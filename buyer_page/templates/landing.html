Consolidation of Purchase Orders

Our platform connects SMEs to a wide range of suppliers and enables them to get the majority of their supplies from this platform. The key idea is to utilize ‘Economies of scale procurement’ to help SMEs to obtain supplies for lower costs. As SMEs usually have smaller orders, the aim of this platform is to consolidate all similar orders from SMEs. This is a win-win situation for both SMEs and suppliers, as SMEs get the same goods for lower prices, and suppliers get large bulk orders which help to grow their business.

Short-term Financing

We provide short-term financing or working capital loans for the SMEs in the form of accounts payable financing. These loans can be balance sheet lending or in collaboration with other alternative lenders such as Aspire or Funding Societies. Alternatively, we also act as a P2P lending firm where investors can invest directly in the working capital loans required by the SME firms on our platform.

Collaboration with TransferWise

We integrate TransferWise API into our payments system, so all transactions are done via TransferWise which automatically converts the currencies to the appropriate target currency. To ensure transparency, all the additional fees involved in the conversion are stated clearly.
